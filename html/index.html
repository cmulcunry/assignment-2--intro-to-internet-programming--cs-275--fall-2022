Rice Recipes

For slightly al dente rice:

1. Combine 1 cups of rice with 1.5 cups of water or broth and 1 Tbsp olive oil.

2. Bring to a boil and stir once to mix.

3. Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes.

4. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.

For softer rice, increase liquid by 0.5 cups and cook time by 5 minutes.

Ratio: 1 cup rice to 1.6 cups water, rounded to the nearest half cup.
