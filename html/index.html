Rice Recipes

Type of rice:
Number of cups:

Combine cup(s) of rice with cups of water and 1 Tbsp olive oil.

Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to the lowest setting.

Cook for about 18 minutes.

For slightly al dente rice: Combine cup(s) of rice with cups of water and 1 Tbsp olive oil.

Bring to a boil and stir once to mix.

Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes.

Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes.

Fluff with a fork and serve.

For softer rice: Increase liquid by 1/2 cup and cook time by 5 minutes.
